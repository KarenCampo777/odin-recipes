Quesadillas

Esasy, cheese and nutricious meal. Make them with simple and fresh ingredients. hether you prefer more traditional ground beef and flank steak quesadillas, or fun twists like veggies, fruits or even exotic delicacies. Try this amazing dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 Butter or oil
  • 8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
  • ½ small yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon of salt & pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Colby cheese
  • 4 mushed garlic cloves
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 avocados

Steps

  1. Stirfry

    Melt butter or heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir onion in melted butter until slightly tender, about 5 minutes. Add ground beef, salt, pepper, and garlic powder; cook and stir until beef is browned and crumbly, about 10 minutes. Remove from the heat, drain liquid from beef, and let sit for 2 minutes.

  2. Stuff tortilla

    Place 1 tortilla on the hot griddle, top with some of the Colby cheese, 1/4 of the beef mixture. Top with more Colby cheese and 1 tortilla. Flatten the quesadilla with your hand.

  3. Brown Quesadillas

    Flip quesadilla with a spatula and cook until browned and cheese is melted,for about 3 to 5 minutes.

  4. Guacamole toping

    Smash avocado, garlic and tomato. add salt, pepper and a bit of chopped cilantro

  5. Serve

    Cut quesadillas in hal or in 4 pieces. Serve with the guacamole topping and sour cream (optional)
